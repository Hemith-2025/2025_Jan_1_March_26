Managed Print Services

Managed Print Services (MPS) is a comprehensive approach to optimizing an organization’s printing and imaging infrastructure while managing the entire document lifecycle. MPS providers help businesses gain better control over their printing and imaging resources, reduce costs, improve efficiency, and enhance document security and sustainability. Here are key aspects and features of Managed Print Services.

  • Printer + Consumables + Service
  • One agreement for all your Office Print requirement
  • Monitor and manage change in your day-to-day business
  • Device supplied as per the requirement
  • Rapid Support
  • Visibility on prints
  • Pay for what you use
  • Ease to control and gain visibility of your printing
  • 'Follow me print solution' offers you the ease of monitoring and controlling your print fleet irrespective of time and place.
  • Take control of your current print environment
  • Reduce “native” printers and make more efficient placement
  • Remote Monitoring
  • Automated supplies supply
  • Improve employee productivity
  • Enhance device and user security
  • Reduce carbon footprint, avoid paper wastage
  • Consolidate costs and vendors—reduce TCO
